Brandon Y. Feng

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Maryland, College Park. I am a member of the UMD Graphics and Visual Informatics Laboratory advised by Prof. Amitabh Varshney. I went to college at the University of Virginia and received my B.A. in 2018 and M.S. in 2019.

My general interests lie at the intersection of deep learning, computer graphics, and VR/AR. I am particularly interested in image/video processing for immersive displays and 3D vision for mixed/extended reality applications. I love learning about applications of machine learning to problems involving capturing and reconstructing 3D structures of any kind.
